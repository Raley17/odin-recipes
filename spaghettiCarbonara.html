Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with creamy eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

    First Recipe Ingredients
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
    Steps
  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the pancetta until crisp.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and mix with grated cheese and pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta and combine with pancetta, then remove from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture until creamy â€” the residual heat will cook the eggs.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.
