Scrambled Eggs

Description

Today you are going to learn how to make fluffy and creamy scrambled eggs!

    First Recipe Ingredients
  • Two eggs
  • Half cup of milk
  • Oil for the pan
  • (Optional) Salt and pepper
    Steps
  1. First Step: Whisk both eggs until it get's bubbly
  2. Add the half cup of milk while whisking
  3. Then start to heat the skillet with some oil and put the eggs right after
  4. As the mixture starts to cook, stir until it start to solidify, once it's completelly solid, serve
