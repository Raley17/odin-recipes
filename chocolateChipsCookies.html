Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Deliciously soft and chewy cookies filled with melty chocolate chips — perfect for any occasion.

    First Recipe Ingredients
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt
    Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until smooth.
  3. Add the egg and mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt until combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Place spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10–12 minutes.
  7. Let cool before serving and enjoy!
